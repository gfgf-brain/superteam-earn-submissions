### What Exactly Are Amulets in the Web3 Context?

At their core, Amulets are **Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs)**. This means each Amulet is a unique digital asset, verifiable on the blockchain, that cannot be replicated or exchanged for another identical item. Unlike fungible tokens (like SOL or USDC) where one unit is interchangeable with another, each Amulet possesses distinct characteristics.

These characteristics are what make Amulets so powerful. They can manifest as:

*   **Stat Boosts:** Enhancing attributes like strength, intelligence, speed, or luck within a game.
*   **Ability Modifiers:** Granting new skills, passive bonuses, or altering existing ones.
*   **Resource Multipliers:** Increasing the yield of in-game resources like wood, ore, or magic crystals.
*   **Cosmetic Enhancements:** Offering unique visual flair and personalization for your in-game avatar or assets.
*   **Access Keys:** Unlocking exclusive areas, quests, or game modes.

The beauty of Amulets lies in their **utility**. They are not merely collectibles; they are functional tools that directly impact a player's experience and their ability to earn within a game's economy. This utility is what distinguishes them from many earlier NFT implementations that focused primarily on speculative value.
